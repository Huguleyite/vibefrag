Eilish Intense

by Billie Eilish Fragrances
Not yet released — sign-up only

Billie's amber gourmand just got louder. Eilish Intense takes the original's DNA — sugared petals, vanilla, musk — and asks it to do more work.

The original Eilish launched in 2021 as an amber gourmand built on sugared petals, vanilla, and musk, and it's carried the line through two flankers since: No. 2 in 2022, No. 3 in 2023. Intense is the fourth stop, and true to the name, it isn't chasing a new direction. It's turning up the one that already works. Billie's own copy frames the goal as richer vanilla, warmer amber, more spice, and deeper woods, aiming for something more enveloping without losing what made the original recognizable.

The pyramid backs that up. It opens on sugared petals, mandarin, and warm berries — brighter and juicier than the original's softer open. The heart holds creamy vanilla, soft spices, and cocoa, and the base settles into sleek woods, warm musks, and tonka bean. Tonka is the tell: it reads richer and more resinous than a standard vanilla-musk base, which is exactly the kind of weight "intense" is supposed to deliver.

It's early. As of this writing, Eilish Intense is sign-up-only on Billie's official fragrance store — no price or release date confirmed, just the notes and description above. It's arriving right behind Your Turn II, which just launched in Billie's other fragrance line, putting the brand in an active release stretch. Worth bookmarking. If the original Eilish's reception is any indicator, this one won't stay quiet for long.

The Line So Far

Four Stops Into the Eilish Namesake Series

Eilish (2021) opened the line as an amber gourmand: sugared petals, vanilla, musk. Eilish No. 2 (2022) went woodier and spicier — bergamot, papyrus, black pepper, palo santo. Eilish No. 3 (2023) shifted fresher and sharper: pink pepper, grapefruit, jasmine, fir, cedar, saffron. Intense doesn't extend the line sideways. It reaches back to the original and pushes it further.
