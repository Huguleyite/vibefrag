Privacy Policy

VibeFrag doesn't ask you to create an account, and we don't collect payment information — every purchase happens on someone else's site. Here's what we do collect, what happens when you click an affiliate link, and what choices you have.

What we collect

This site is hosted on GitHub Pages. When you visit vibefrag.com, GitHub's servers log standard request data — your IP address, browser type, and the pages you visit. This is governed by GitHub's Privacy Statement.

We also use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use vibefrag.com — pages viewed, general location, device type, and referral source. Read Google's privacy policy, or opt out of Google Analytics tracking here. Nothing on this site asks for your email or any personal information.

Affiliate links

VibeFrag earns a small commission when you buy through certain links on this site. Once you click through to a retailer, you're on their site, subject to their own privacy policy and cookies — not ours.

Our primary retail partner is BeautyHouse.com (operated by Scent Lux Inc.), a Shopify store. BeautyHouse collects device information — via cookies, log files, and pixels — when you browse, and order information (name, address, payment details) if you buy. They share data with Shopify and Google Analytics. Read their full policy at beautyhouse.com/policies/privacy-policy, where you can also opt out of Google Analytics tracking.

We also link to Jomashop, Amazon (via the Amazon Associates program), and other retailers from time to time. Each has its own privacy practices, which we don't control — check the retailer's own policy before you buy.

What we don't do

We do not sell, rent, or share personal information — we don't have any to share beyond the standard site analytics above. We don't run our own behavioral advertising. Any ads or retargeting you see after visiting a retailer's site come from that retailer, not from VibeFrag.

Your choices

You can block or clear cookies in your browser, and opt out of Google Analytics using the link above. Most retailer sites, including BeautyHouse, offer their own cookie and ad-tracking opt-outs in their privacy policy.
