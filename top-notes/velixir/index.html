Velixir

Fresh. Bold. Limitless.

Velixir Parfums was founded in Indonesia in 2025, built on a straightforward premise: accessible luxury shouldn't be a contradiction. The house crafts every release around craftsmanship and quality first, an approach it hopes carries Indonesian perfumery to a global stage. Icarus, Asteria, Poseidon, Persephone. The catalog reads like a syllabus. Enough of it sells out that "sold out" has become part of the brand's identity. These three are where to start.
